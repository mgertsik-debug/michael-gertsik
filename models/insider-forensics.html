WHAT THE TRADE-TIME SIGNALS MEAN
Outsized vs market (size)
The trade is 3+ standard deviations larger than the other trades in this same market right now.
Lone whale (whale)
10×+ the 90th-percentile trade on this market — it dwarfs everyone else betting here.
News blackout (blackout)
Zero global news matched the market's topic in the hours before this bet — betting big before the public has any news is the information-asymmetry tell. Weighted highest.
Federal Register (fedReg)
A recent regulatory document's title matches the market's exact subject. Low weight, corroborator only.
Status
watching = open, unresolved · promoted = resolved in the bettor's favour AND the account is now a flagged suspect (hardened into a case) · cleared = resolved without panning out (self-cleared).
