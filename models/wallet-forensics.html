A PATTERN, NOT A VERDICT

This shows a winning record extraordinarily unlikely to be luck. It does not prove who these people are, what they knew, or that they broke the law. Confirming that requires records only the exchange or a regulator can obtain. Everyone is presumed innocent. This is an investigative lead, not legal advice.

Methodology inspired by the peer-reviewed prediction-market insider-trading research it builds on: the Harvard Law School composite-signal study (93,000 Polymarket markets, March 2026) and the London Business School & Yale "expert winners" study (1.72M accounts, 2025). This tool operationalizes and extends their five-signal framework with exact per-wallet binomial improbability, de-correlation, and on-chain funding-graph clustering. All trade data is pulled from Polymarket's public APIs.
