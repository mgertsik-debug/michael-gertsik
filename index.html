My work sits at the intersection of law, emerging technologies, and digital asset markets — a space I've studied closely since 2021, when the rapid rise of NFTs first revealed how decentralized systems could reshape how markets form, how information spreads, and how value moves across networks.

What began as late-night deep-dives into smart contracts, governance structures, and trading protocols gradually became a clear professional direction: understanding and helping shape the legal frameworks governing this evolving space.
